About

L. Michael Turvin, AIA is an architect and systems thinker working across practice, digital tooling, and workflow minimalism. Turvin Design Lab is the R&D arm of the practice—experiments in computation, visualization, and operations.

Currently Project Architect with Cuningham Group, Michael leads exterior architecture for a major U.S. theme park development (CA phase) and a new technology campus training facility (in design).
