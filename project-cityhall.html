Civic / City Hall

New Hope, MN — Completed 2022

Project Image Placeholder

The New Hope City Hall and Police Station brings civic transparency and human-scale design to a growing suburban community. The building combines administrative offices, council chambers, and police functions around a public plaza.

Role: Project Architect — design development, coordination, and construction documentation. Emphasis on daylighting, public interface, and efficient modular façade logic.
